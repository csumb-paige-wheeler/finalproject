Academic Resources

Assist.org is a website that provides official transfer information and articulation agreements between California Community Colleges and the University of California (UC) and California State University (CSU) systems. It allows students to see how courses taken at one institution will transfer to another and helps them plan their academic pathway towards a bachelor's degree.

Chegg is an online learning platform that provides various services such as textbook rentals, online tutoring, and homework help to college students. It offers a subscription-based model and aims to make education more accessible and affordable for students around the world.

Citation Machine is a popular website that helps students and researchers create accurate citations in a variety of formats, such as APA, MLA, and Chicago style. The website offers a user-friendly interface and helpful resources to ensure that citations are formatted correctly and meet the requirements of academic writing.

Khan Academy is a non-profit educational website that provides free online courses and educational resources for learners of all ages. The website offers a wide range of subjects, including math, science, humanities, and computer programming, and features video tutorials, practice exercises, and interactive quizzes. Khan Academy is widely recognized for its high-quality content and has helped millions of learners worldwide to improve their knowledge and skills.

Tutor.com is an online tutoring service that provides one-on-one tutoring sessions with professional tutors in various subjects, including math, science, English, and social studies. The website offers flexible scheduling options and 24/7 access to a wide range of academic resources, such as study guides and practice tests. Tutor.com is a popular choice for students of all ages and has helped thousands of learners to achieve their academic goals.

"Rate My Professor" is a popular website where students can anonymously rate and review their professors based on factors such as teaching style, course difficulty, and overall helpfulness. The website also provides helpful information on professors' backgrounds and allows students to search for instructors based on specific criteria.
